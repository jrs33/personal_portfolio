About Me

When I was a kid, I knew I wanted to make a difference like my Mom or Dad had in the world. It was always incredible to hear of my Dads stories as a firefighter/EMT saving people, or my Moms stories as a Health Unit Coordinator, creating a environment to make it easier for doctors to care for their patients. I knew I wanted to use my math and science ability to make my very own impact on healthcare, and wanted to some day start my own initiative to make my mark.

Fast forward to my college years, my parents were ecstatic as I entered Boston University as a first generation college student in their highly ranked Biomedical Engineering program. I went through my first year, and found a love for programming; a great application of my creativity, and engineering ability, and a cool new way to impact archaic healthcare.

Setting these skills into motion, I jumped into some great research positions to get my feet wet. I interned as the only undergrad on a team of surgeons and bioinformatic scientists at MGH, employing Python to target metabolic changes in livers during transplant to aid in drug development. I continued this work at BU my junior year as an intern in an on-campus bioinformatics lab, and switched over to my current Computer Engineering major.

However, I still felt as though I was not making as big of an impact as I'd have liked. It seemed as though my work in academia wasn't bringing me close enough to the healthcare industry I grew up wanting to impact. Disenchanted with research a bit, I applied for industry internships my junior year, and landed a spot at Optum as a Data Engineer.

This is precisely the point in time I write to you today. I want to continue my story and growth in the digital health industry and, in the short term, hope to further data-related projects in a company that will push my engineering skills to the next level, with a long term goal of using my knowledge to further advance digital health initiatives, whether they are my own or a respected peers. I can add value in projects given my experience with tools like:

  • Python
  • C/C++
  • Java
  • SQL
  • Hadoop
  • Spark

Thank you for taking some time to get to know me, and I encourage you to jump over to my Projects page to read more about some of the work I do. Also feel free to download my Resume to further check out my qualifications, and check out my Contact page to get in touch with me if you think I would be a good addition to your company/project, or if you just want to chat.
