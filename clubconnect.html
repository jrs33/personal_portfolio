ClubConnect

        
Empowering students and club leaders to connect via a simple, clean social network 

        
Description


At BU there is a thriving extracurricular community. However, the central organization that oversees the clubs can sometimes be tough to deal with, and the software they utilize is very ugly and inconvenient.Thus, four of my friends and I decided to tackle this problem in a semester long app project. We call it ClubConnect, and the aim is to allow students to easily search, add, and connect with all clubs.
 


Role

 
As the Project Lead, I oversaw everything from authentication employment, to full stack development and the utilization of the remote storage Firebase tool to store our data. It was, by far, one of my favorite projects I have done so far, and we are working on getting it developed further on campus.
  



Tools Used


Android Studio, Java, Firebase, JSON, XML
