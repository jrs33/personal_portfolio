InnovateEDU

        
Bringing together student entrepreneurs from Emerson, MIT, NEU and BU for a semester to compete

        
Description


Aside from my engineering interests, I also have an interest in Entrepreneurship, and taking engineering innovations to market. As the Vice President of the Entrepreneurship Club at BU, I have found this as my 'business education outlet' and a great place to get inspiration and also help out students with similar interests. With InnovateEDU, I am serving as one of the four liasons between MIT, Northeastern, Emerson and BU to create the first cross-campus entrepreneurship competition. It will be an intensive, semester long program to give elite students a chance to ideate an build a product and pitch at the end for prize money. 
 


Role


In my role, I have helped get sponsorship, and also have selected the BU team, and worked with the other schools in Boston to make sure all of the events are planned properly and effectively. I am very excited for the launch semester in Spring 2017, and look forward to bringing on more schools in subsequent semesters to grow the impact.
