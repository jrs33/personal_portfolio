Startup Challenge

        
The largest introductory program for entrepreneurship at BU with an aim to foster talent and spark interest

        
Description


In my involvement in the Entrepreneurship Club, I also oversee the Startup Challenge, which is a major competition at BU that gives students with little exposure to starting a company an introduction in a semester long program. We work to bring in speakers and entrepreneurs around Boston to give students at BU an introduction to entrepreneurship (ideation, market research, product development), and prepare them for future accelerators. Ultimately, the program culminates in a pitchathon competition where teams pitch for a chance to win a cash prize to further pursue their idea.
 


Role


In my role as VP, I correspond with the MBA community on campus, as well as the Venture Accelerator, to be sure we are designing a curriculum that prepares students to move on to these programs. I also work with the interns within our club to make sure the program runs smoothly, and love giving students even better opportunities and more refined programs compared to my earlier years at BU.
