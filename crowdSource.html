Welcome to Crowdsourcing!

What I love most about software is collaboration. Many of the most interesting projects I've worked on have stemmed from friends or mentors simply sending me a link to an interesting article, tutorial, blog post, video, etc. I have found this to be essential to my growth as an engineer, and felt a great need to replicate these exchanges here on my CrowdSourcing page. If you have any interesting web content you've come across, I invite you to post the info in the specified form, and also check out some of the links others have posted. Cheers!
